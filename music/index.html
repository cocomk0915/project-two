City by music

In order to learn more about someone, I think music is one of the perfect topics to start with. So, I want to share my passion and love of music with you here. From RnB, Disco, to gospel, music became a big part of my life. I like to listen to music so much that I often can think of certain music that goes well with paintings, brands, or even objects. As I lived in four different cities so far, there are some music that reminds me of each city. Meeting people who have various genre of music in different cities was a big joy for me. Music has always been there to uplift my mood, make me cry, complete assignments to, get inspired, and dance. With music, I felt more alive. Here are some of my favorite songs that reminds me of each city I have lived. Hope you enjoy! :)
